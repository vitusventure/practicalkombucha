        Finishing first 1F in a year

        October 8, 2022
        Log entry
        base kombucha recipe, simple syrup 1, dry hopped, bottling

        1F from year old starter finished toda after 8 days fermeting (Base Kombucha Recipe). Final Ph 2.5, nicely tart. Started another batch using full gallon recipe. Bottled 2 bottles with Simple Syrup 1. Added remaining 1/2 gallon to large mason jar with .5oz Cashmere hops (pellets, added loose) for dry hopping experiment.
